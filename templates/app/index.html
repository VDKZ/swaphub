How does it work ?

First you create your account and set your website link to be promoted

Then you earn points browsing other members websites

Note that if you use the speed mode you will receive 4x more points

And then what ?

Other members will do the same and browse your website to earn points

Basically if you use speed mode for 1 hour you will receive arround 100 visit on your link(s)

Rules

Friend click is 100% free and does not use bot to visit your link(s)

You can't buy points (to keep balance between members points and visits)

If you reach 0 points, your link(s) won't get visited until you earn points
