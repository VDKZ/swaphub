Support
How can i earn points ?
To earn points, you must visit links in the dashboard section. You can use the x4 mode to earn more points, the more you visit links, the more points you earn.
How long it take to receive visits ?
It depend on when the other members will visit your link(s), keep in mind that the more points you have, the higher you will be in the link list.
How can i now if the visits are delivered to my website ?
You can install tracker such as google analytics to see the statistics about received visits.
How much website traffic can SwapHub deliver?
This depend on the availability of the other members and the daily visits limit you have configured on your account.
What is the Visit Duration?
The visit duration is a random value between 1 minute and 4 minutes so the average visit will be 2,5 minutes.
Why i don't receive any visits ?
When you have 0 points or less, your links are no longer visible in the dashboard. Earn points in order to get visits on your link(s).
